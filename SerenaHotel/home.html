About us

Dear Guest, I have been asked many times, why in 1994 I established Serena Resorts. The answer is simple, tourism is our future. I therefore decided to start and offer something special to that future, which is why being special is what Serena Resorts is all about. Through operating city hotel and resort properties, it is our policy to offer a location that is unique. Consequently our properties can be found in authentic local villages, by beautiful clean beaches rich with marine life, near sites of touristic interest and in prime business locations. Staffed by accomplished hoteliers, marketing, training and finance specialists with creative, innovative and entrepreneurial strengths and experience, our team provides the expertise and experience for new and preexisting projects. With our high quality of service and dedication towards guest satisfaction, a reliable return on investment is the fundamental rationale of all Serena business.
